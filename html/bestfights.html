Best fights

Jones vs gustaffson 1

jon Jones vs Alexandar gustaffson 1

The fight between Jon Jones and Alexander Gustafsson took place at UFC 165 in 2013. Gustafsson surprised with his striking skills, utilizing his reach advantage against Jones. However, Jones adjusted and showcased his resilience, ultimately winning a unanimous decision after a back-and-forth battle. The fight is considered one of the greatest light heavyweight bouts in UFC history.

Rory vs lawler 2

rory macdonald vs robbie lawler 2

The rematch between Rory MacDonald and Robbie Lawler at UFC 189 was a highly intense welterweight title fight. Both fighters displayed their skills, but Lawler's relentless pressure and powerful strikes proved decisive. In the fourth round, Lawler delivered a devastating left hook that caused significant damage to MacDonald's nose. Despite his determination, the ringside doctor stopped the fight, giving Lawler a TKO victory and allowing him to retain the title. The bout is considered one of the greatest in the division's history.

Tony vs gaethje

Tony ferguson vs justin gaethje

At UFC 249, Justin Gaethje defeated Tony Ferguson by TKO in the fifth round, winning the interim lightweight title. Gaethje showcased his striking skills and power, while Ferguson displayed his durability and heart. The fight was highly praised for its action-packed nature and marked Gaethje's rise in the division.

porier vs max

Dustin porier vs max holloway

At UFC 236 on April 13, 2019, Max Holloway and Dustin Poirier fought for the interim lightweight championship. Poirier countered Holloway's aggressive striking with powerful combinations. The fight was competitive with back-and-forth exchanges. Poirier's ability to absorb punishment and deliver impactful strikes earned him a unanimous decision victory after five rounds, securing the interim lightweight title.

kevin vs izzy

kevin gastelum vs israel adesanya

At UFC 236, Kelvin Gastelum and Israel Adesanya fought for the interim UFC middleweight championship. It was an intense back-and-forth battle, with Adesanya showcasing his striking skills and Gastelum displaying his toughness. Adesanya's precision and ability to maintain distance proved decisive. Despite Gastelum's resilience, Adesanya won by unanimous decision, securing the interim title. The fight is regarded as one of the best middleweight bouts in UFC history. watch the fight
